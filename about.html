ABOUT JULIA MOONCAKE

Julia Moon Cake started from a family kitchen, inspired by the Mid-Autumn Festival's spirit of reunion and gratitude. Each mooncake is handcrafted with care, using time-honored recipes and quality ingredients to bring warmth and nostalgia to every bite.

MEET JULIA

Julia’s passion for baking and sharing traditional desserts inspired the brand. Her dream is to keep the Mid-Autumn tradition alive while adding a modern twist that everyone can enjoy.

HER CRAFT

Every mooncake is baked in small batches to ensure the perfect balance of flavor and texture. From the silky lotus paste to the golden crust, each step reflects patience and passion.
